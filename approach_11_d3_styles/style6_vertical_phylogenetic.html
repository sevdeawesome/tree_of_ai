This visualization shows the complete evolutionary history of artificial intelligence from 1958 to 2025, following the structure of biological phylogenetic trees.

Reading the tree:

  • Time flows from center (1958) outward (2025)
  • Branches show ancestry and divergence
  • Branch thickness = importance
  • Dashed lines = extinct lineages
